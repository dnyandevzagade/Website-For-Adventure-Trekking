Himalayan Base Camp Trek

5 Days | Himalayas, India

About This Trek

Embark on an unforgettable journey to the base camp of the majestic Himalayas. This 5-day trek will take you through breathtaking landscapes, remote villages, and stunning mountain vistas. Our expert guides will lead you safely through the trails while sharing their knowledge of the local culture and environment.

Trek Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at base village, orientation, and short acclimatization walk.

Day 2: Trek through rhododendron forests to the first campsite (8km, 5-6 hours).

Day 3: Ascend to higher altitude, passing through shepherd trails (10km, 6-7 hours).

Day 4: Summit day - early morning climb to base camp (5km, 4-5 hours).

Day 5: Return to base village and departure.

What to Carry

  • Sturdy trekking shoes
  • Warm clothing (thermal wear, fleece jacket)
  • Rainproof jacket and pants
  • Sun protection (hat, sunglasses, sunscreen)
  • Water bottles (2 liters capacity)
  • Personal medications and first aid
  • Headlamp or flashlight
  • Trekking poles (optional but recommended)

Safety Rules

  • Follow the guide's instructions at all times
  • Stay with the group and don't wander off
  • Maintain proper hydration throughout the trek
  • Inform the guide immediately if feeling unwell
  • Respect the local environment - no littering
  • No alcohol consumption during the trek
